# RiskMind

## What This Is

RiskMind is an AI-native multi-organization enterprise risk management platform that provides comprehensive risk identification, assessment, and mitigation. It features risk registers, third-party risk management (TPRM) with a 7-state vendor lifecycle, compliance framework tracking, AI-driven signal triage and enrichment, and an autonomous risk intelligence agent. Built as a TypeScript monorepo with Express 5 API, React + Vite frontend, PostgreSQL with pgvector, and multi-tenant RBAC. Originally developed on Replit, now being migrated to a dedicated server for internal use.

## Core Value

A working, demo-ready enterprise risk management platform that an internal organization can use to manage real risks, vendors, and compliance — powered by AI enrichment and accessible via a public Cloudflare tunnel URL.
